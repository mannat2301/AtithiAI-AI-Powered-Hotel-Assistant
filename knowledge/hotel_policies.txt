Marriott Hotel – Guest Guidelines, Policies, and Service Information

Welcome to Marriott Hotels. The following guidelines and policies are provided to ensure a safe, comfortable, and enjoyable stay for all guests. Please review the information below for details regarding hotel services, facilities, and guest responsibilities.

--------------------------------------------------

CHECK-IN AND CHECK-OUT

Check-in Time:
Guests may check in starting from 3:00 PM.

Check-out Time:
Guests must check out by 12:00 PM (noon).

Early Check-in / Late Check-out:
Early check-in or late check-out may be available depending on room availability and may incur additional charges.

--------------------------------------------------

FRONT DESK / RECEPTION

Reception Service:
The hotel front desk operates 24 hours a day.

Guest Assistance:
Guests may contact the front desk at any time for assistance with inquiries, service requests, or general support.

--------------------------------------------------

IDENTIFICATION AND PAYMENT

Identification Requirement:
All guests must present a valid government-issued photo ID during check-in.

Payment:
A credit or debit card may be required to complete payment and cover incidental charges during the stay.

--------------------------------------------------

CANCELLATION AND NO-SHOW POLICY

Cancellation Terms:
Cancellation policies may vary depending on the booking type and selected rate plan.

Free Cancellation:
Some bookings allow free cancellation within a specified time period before arrival.

Late Cancellation or No-Show:
Late cancellations or failure to arrive may result in applicable charges according to the booking terms.

--------------------------------------------------

WI-FI AND INTERNET ACCESS

Wi-Fi Availability:
Complimentary wireless internet access is available for all registered hotel guests.

Service Hours:
Wi-Fi is available 24 hours a day throughout the property.

--------------------------------------------------

HOUSEKEEPING SERVICES

Housekeeping Hours:
Daily housekeeping service is available between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM.

Service Options:
Guests may request reduced housekeeping service or opt out of housekeeping if preferred.

--------------------------------------------------

BREAKFAST SERVICE

Breakfast Hours:
Breakfast is served between 7:30 AM and 10:30 AM.

Availability:
Breakfast availability may depend on the room type or booking plan selected by the guest.

--------------------------------------------------

FITNESS CENTER

Operating Hours:
The fitness center is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Guest Responsibility:
Guests are expected to follow all posted safety instructions when using the fitness facilities.

--------------------------------------------------

SWIMMING POOL

Pool Hours:
The swimming pool is open from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Usage Rules:
Pool access may be limited based on safety guidelines or occupancy capacity.

--------------------------------------------------

SMOKING POLICY

Non-Smoking Areas:
Smoking is strictly prohibited inside guest rooms and indoor hotel areas.

Designated Smoking Areas:
Outdoor smoking areas may be available at designated locations.

Policy Violations:
Cleaning fees or penalties may apply if smoking occurs in non-smoking areas.

--------------------------------------------------

PET POLICY

Pet Permissions:
Pet policies may vary depending on the property location.

Fees and Restrictions:
Where pets are allowed, additional fees or restrictions may apply.

Guests should contact the front desk for property-specific pet policies.

--------------------------------------------------

QUIET HOURS

Quiet Hours Policy:
Quiet hours are enforced between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM.

Guest Expectations:
Guests are requested to minimize noise during these hours to ensure comfort for other guests.

--------------------------------------------------

SAFETY AND SECURITY

Personal Belongings:
Guests are responsible for securing their personal belongings during their stay.

Hotel Liability:
The hotel is not responsible for lost, unattended, or misplaced items.

Emergency Preparedness:
Guests are encouraged to review emergency exits and safety instructions upon arrival.

--------------------------------------------------

GUEST CONDUCT

Respectful Behavior:
Guests are expected to treat hotel staff and other guests with respect.

Policy Violations:
The hotel reserves the right to refuse service or terminate a guest's stay if hotel policies are violated.

--------------------------------------------------

ADDITIONAL ASSISTANCE

For property-specific information, special requests, or further assistance, guests may contact the front desk at any time.
